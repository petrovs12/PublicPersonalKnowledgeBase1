$var(v) = \Sigma(v_i-E(v))^2 = E[(X-\mu)^2]$

$cov(X,Y) = \Sigma((x_i-E(x))(y_i-E(y)))/n$  

or (n-1) for sample covariance

$cor(X,Y) = \frac{cov(X,Y)}{\sqrt{var(X)*var(Y)}}$
